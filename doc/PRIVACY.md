Privacy Policy for Batch OCR Alltime
Last updated: April 28, 2026
1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the Batch OCR Alltime application (“the App”). The App is designed to perform all OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and image processing operations entirely on the user’s local device.
2. Information We Do NOT Collect
The App does not collect, store, process, or transmit any of the following information:
Personal information (such as name, email address, phone number, or location).
User-uploaded images, documents, or text content processed by the OCR feature.
Usage data, analytics, or device identifiers.
Any other form of personally identifiable or sensitive information.
All data related to your files and OCR tasks remains exclusively on your device and is never sent to external servers, third parties, or us.
3. Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics tools that would collect or transmit user data.
4. Children’s Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect or solicit information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.
5. Your Rights
Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no user data we hold to access, correct, or delete. All data processed by the App remains under your full control on your device.
